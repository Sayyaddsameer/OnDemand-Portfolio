About Me

I'm a final-year Computer Science student at Aditya University, an AWS Certified Developer – Associate and RHCSA-certified engineer who builds cloud-native backend systems. What genuinely pulls me in is serverless, event-driven architecture on AWS — taking a vague problem statement and turning it into a system that scales cleanly, fails gracefully, and costs almost nothing while it sits idle.

Most of my building happens in Python and FastAPI, containerized with Docker and wired together with services like Lambda, Step Functions, API Gateway, DynamoDB and SES. I care about the parts that never show up in a demo — idempotency, eager-loaded queries, zero-trust IAM, cold-start times — because that's the gap between a project that looks finished and one that actually holds up under load. I sharpen the fundamentals behind all of it through 200+ solved DSA problems, and I'm looking for a role where I can keep shipping real cloud infrastructure.

CloudSentinel AI — Multi-Cloud Security & GenAI Platform

PROBLEM: Cloud-security tools bury teams in context-free alerts and scan domains one after another, so genuine threats surface slowly and remediation lags behind.

SOLUTION: A serverless, multi-cloud platform that runs five security-domain scanners in parallel and hands every finding to an LLM for grounded, plain-language remediation.

KEY IMPLEMENTATION: Orchestrated five scanners concurrently with AWS Step Functions (3.47× faster than sequential), with a Groq-hosted Llama 3.3 model writing grounded remediation under a strict zero-trust IAM model. A live AWS scan reached 96.6% precision (84/87 findings, zero AI hallucinations), spanning AWS + GCP with fully Terraform-defined infrastructure.

Step Functions Lambda Bedrock Groq · Llama 3.3 DynamoDB Cognito Comprehend GCP Terraform
